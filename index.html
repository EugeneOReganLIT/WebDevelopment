IDEs

If you are using a text editor (be it NotePad, NotePad++ or Brackets (INSERT LINKS)) and are comfortable, carry on. However, if you wish to use an IDE (either NetBeans or Eclipse preferably), it will be beneficial in the long term. IDEs have very specific funcionality to assist the programmer.
